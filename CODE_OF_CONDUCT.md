# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — contributors and maintainers — pledge to make taking part in this project a
welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, visible
or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual orientation. We pledge to act in ways that keep the community open,
respectful, and healthy.

## Our standards

Behaviour that helps the community includes:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility for our mistakes, apologising to those affected, and
  learning from the experience.
- Focusing on what is best for the community as a whole.

Behaviour that is not acceptable includes:

- Harassment of any kind, whether public or private.
- Insulting, derogatory, or demeaning comments, and personal or political
  attacks.
- Unwelcome attention or advances of a personal nature.
- Publishing other people's private information, such as a physical or email
  address, without their explicit permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues,
pull requests, and discussions — and whenever someone is representing the
project in a public space.

## Enforcement

Concerns about behaviour covered here can be reported to the project maintainer
at **santonicosmo@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly,
and the maintainer will respect the privacy of the person who raised the report.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and will take fair
corrective action — from a private warning, to a temporary restriction, to a
permanent ban — in response to behaviour they judge inappropriate.
